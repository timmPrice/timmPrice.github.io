FAQ's

  • Is this battle bots?

    Nope, its much more collaborative. We work together rather than destroy each other. Check out our robots page for more info

  • How can I join?

    Join our Discord server and make a post in the "#Introductions" channel and show up for a meeting. Anyone is welcome

  • Is it too late in the semseter to join?

    Nope, join us any Monday or Wednesday, help yourself to some pizza, and enjoy building awesome robots

  • If I join, what will I be doing?

    We people interested in building robots, programming both driver control and autonomous functions, people to work on with our media / logbook, or really anyone willing with interest and willingness to learn!
